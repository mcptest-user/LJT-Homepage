SynLogic is a framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale to facilitate learning logical reasoning and beyond. This work addresses the challenge of obtaining high-quality reasoning data for training language models by providing a systematic approach to generate diverse and verifiable reasoning examples.
